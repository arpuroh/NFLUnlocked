Week 12: The Gibbs Explosion & The War Begins

Week 12 will be remembered for exactly one thing: Jahmyr Gibbs going absolutely nuclear for ShakeNBake. In a performance that will be talked about for years, Gibbs dropped a staggering 52.90 points (219 rushing yards, multiple TDs), propelling ShakeNBake to a league-high 166.30 points. It wasn't just a fantasy performance; it was a hostile takeover. This single-handedly ended Poop Squad's brief flicker of hope and served notice to the rest of the league that the Indian faction is bringing heavy artillery to the Thanksgiving showdown.

Meanwhile, The Injured Reserved (Greg Gagne) continued his dominant march, posting 146 points to remain the league's points leader. Hail Mary (Andrew Barnhart) casually dropped another 150-burger to stay atop the standings at 20-4. But the most surprising development might be Bend The Knee (Darrius), who seems to have finally woken up, scoring nearly 149 points to play spoiler. As we head into Week 13, the lines are drawn. Cowboys vs. Indians. The projections favor the Indians by over 30 points, but if we learned anything from Week 12, it's that chaos is the only constant.
